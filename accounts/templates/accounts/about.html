Learn More About Omar

Hi, I'm Omar El-Mahdy, an online fitness coach and nutrition specialist with over 4 years of experience in transforming lives. My journey began with a passion for fitness and a desire to help others achieve their health and wellness goals. Over the years, I've had the privilege of working with more than 1,000 women from all walks of life, each with unique challenges and aspirations.

I specialize in female training and nutrition, with a deep understanding of women's health issues such as PCOS, thyroid disorders, insulin resistance, and postpartum recovery. My approach is holistic, focusing not just on physical transformation but also on mental well-being and building a positive relationship with food.

What sets me apart is my commitment to personalized care. I work closely with a dedicated medical team, including a physiotherapist, pharmacist, and medical doctor, to ensure that every client receives the best possible support. Whether you're dealing with a specific health condition or simply looking to improve your overall fitness, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

With over 10 certifications in nutrition, training, and mental health, I bring a wealth of knowledge to my coaching. My mission is to empower you to become the strongest, healthiest, and most confident version of yourself. Let's embark on this journey together and create lasting change!

Frequently Asked Questions About Online Coaching

How does online coaching work?

Online coaching is designed to fit your lifestyle. After signing up, you'll receive a personalized training and nutrition plan tailored to your goals. We'll stay in touch through regular check-ins, video calls, and messaging to track your progress and make adjustments as needed.

Can I join if I have a medical condition?

Absolutely! I specialize in working with women who have conditions like PCOS, thyroid disorders, insulin resistance, and more. With the support of my medical team, we'll create a safe and effective plan tailored to your needs.

What if I'm a beginner?

No problem at all! My programs are designed for all fitness levels. Whether you're just starting out or looking to take your training to the next level, I'll guide you step by step to ensure you achieve your goals safely and effectively.

How long until I see results?

Results vary depending on your goals, commitment, and starting point. However, most clients start noticing positive changes in their energy levels and overall well-being within the first few weeks, with more visible results within 2-3 months.

Do I need special equipment?

Not at all! My programs are designed to be flexible and can be done with minimal equipment. Whether you're at home or in a gym, I'll provide modifications to suit your available resources.

What makes your coaching different?

My coaching focuses on a holistic approach—combining fitness, nutrition, and mental well-being. With over 10 certifications and a dedicated medical team, I provide personalized support to help you achieve lasting results and build a positive relationship with your body and food.
